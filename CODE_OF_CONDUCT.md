## Code of Conduct

Welcome to Kaido's RPG Project! We want this project to be **friendly, safe, and fun for everyone**, so all contributors, players, and community members must follow these rules:

1. **Be respectful** – Treat everyone with kindness, even if you disagree.  
2. **No harassment** – This includes bullying, threats, stalking, or unwanted attention.  
3. **No swearing or offensive language** – Keep communication clean and friendly.  
4. **No discrimination** – Do not harass anyone based on race, gender, age, religion, disability, sexuality, or neurodivergence.  
5. **No trolling or intentionally causing chaos** – Don’t make work harder for others on purpose.  
6. **No sharing personal information** – Do not post addresses, phone numbers, or other private info.  
7. **No hacking or malicious code** – Do not try to break the project, steal code, or create viruses.  
8. **No spamming** – Avoid posting irrelevant or repeated messages, pull requests, or issues.  
9. **No stealing work** – Credit all code, art, and contributions appropriately.  
10. **No NSFW content** – This includes images, text, or links that are sexual, gory, or violent.  
11. **Be patient** – Project maintainers may take time to respond; don’t harass or pressure them.  
12. **Follow contribution rules** – Only submit changes through the proper GitHub workflow.  
13. **No inappropriate jokes or memes** – Keep humor safe and non-offensive.  
14. **Respect boundaries** – If someone says “stop,” stop immediately.  
15. **No impersonation** – Do not pretend to be another contributor or Kaido.  
16. **Be inclusive** – Encourage everyone to participate and help others learn.  
17. **Do not bypass rules** – Trying to get around these rules will not be tolerated.  
18. **Report issues responsibly** – Use clear, polite messages when reporting bugs or problems.  
19. **Respect decisions of maintainers** – Kaido’s decisions are final for this project.  
20. **Have fun safely** – This project is for learning, creating, and enjoying code—do not ruin it for others.  

Violating these rules may result in **removal from the project or blocked access**. Let’s keep the community safe and enjoyable for **everyone**!
## Additional Rules for Safety of Minors

21. **No slurs or offensive language targeting minors** – Absolutely no age-based insults.  
22. **No sexualized content directed at minors** – Under no circumstances.  
23. **No encouraging dangerous activities** – Includes self-harm, unsafe stunts, or risky challenges.  
24. **No grooming or predatory behavior** – Anyone targeting minors will be reported.  
25. **No sharing adult or explicit material** – Keep all content safe for teens.  
26. **No harassment through private messages** – Includes Discord, email, or GitHub messaging.  
27. **No asking minors for personal information** – Never request full names, addresses, or school info.  
28. **No threats of violence** – Physical, digital, or emotional threats are forbidden.  
29. **No bullying based on age or experience** – Everyone starts somewhere.  
30. **No encouraging illegal activity** – Drugs, hacking, theft, or anything illegal.  
31. **No discriminatory jokes about minors** – Race, gender, religion, neurodivergence, etc.  
32. **No spamming or trolling minors** – Respect young contributors’ time and attention.  
33. **No using minors’ work without permission** – Always ask and give credit.  
34. **No intimidation or scare tactics** – Don’t threaten minors to get code, access, or attention.  
35. **No unsafe links or downloads** – Do not share malware, viruses, or unsafe software.  
36. **No online bullying** – Includes ridicule, mockery, or shaming minors.  
37. **No encouraging unsafe mental challenges** – Avoid content that pressures minors emotionally.  
38. **No targeting minors in marketing or promotion** – Respect privacy and consent.  
39. **No doxxing or revealing minors’ identities** – Ever.  
40. **Report violations immediately** – Any minor-targeting abuse should be reported to Kaido right away.  

Following these rules keeps the project **safe, fun, and friendly for everyone**, especially young creators!
